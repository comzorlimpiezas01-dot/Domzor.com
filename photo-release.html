Photo & Video Authorization

Documentation rights and separate optional consent for marketing use.

Draft notice: This document is a business template and should be reviewed by an Illinois-licensed attorney before production use.

1. Job documentation

Customer authorizes DOMZOR to photograph or record work areas, property conditions, equipment, readings, progress, completed work, and relevant communications for estimating, documentation, quality control, safety, insurance, training, dispute prevention, and legal compliance.

2. Optional marketing consent

Marketing use is optional and is not required to receive service. A customer may allow DOMZOR to use non-identifying before-and-after images in advertising, social media, websites, proposals, and training materials.

3. Identifying information

DOMZOR should not intentionally publish names, faces, addresses, license plates, personal documents, account numbers, or other identifying information without separate express authorization.

4. Withdrawal

A customer may withdraw future marketing consent by contacting DOMZOR. Withdrawal does not require removal of materials already lawfully printed or distributed before DOMZOR received the request.
