DOMZOR TERMS & CONDITIONS V1

WHAT THIS PACKAGE ADDS
- terms.html
- terms.css
- terms.js
- booking gate in pricing.html
- acceptance validation in pricing.js

HOW IT WORKS
1. Customer opens pricing.html.
2. Customer must open terms.html.
3. Customer enters name, email, phone and checks required consent boxes.
4. Acceptance is saved in that browser using localStorage.
5. Customer returns to the booking form.
6. Booking request can then be sent through WhatsApp.

IMPORTANT LIMITATIONS
- localStorage is not a secure server record.
- this version does not email a PDF.
- it does not record IP address or preserve evidence across devices.
- it does not verify identity.
- it does not replace a project-specific written estimate or work order.
- it does not automatically deliver the Illinois consumer-rights pamphlet or federal cancellation notice.

BEFORE USING WITH REAL CUSTOMERS
1. Replace the company name with the exact approved LLC legal name.
2. Add the real business street address.
3. Have an Illinois-licensed attorney review these terms.
4. For applicable home repair/remodeling work over $1,000, use the required written contract/work order and deliver the Illinois “Home Repair: Know Your Consumer Rights” pamphlet and acknowledgment.
5. For covered off-premises sales, provide the required federal cancellation notice.
6. Connect a secure backend or e-signature service to retain audit records and email PDFs.
